## Expanded

一个会自动占满 `Row`, `Column`可用空间的组件。等价于 `flex: 1`

## Usage

### Basic

<Playground>
  <Container width="100%">
    <Row>
      <Container width="100px" height="100px" color="#ececec">
        <Center>居中</Center>
      </Container>
      <Expanded style={{background: "coral"}}>
        <Center>
          <Container width="100px" height="100px" color="blue">
            <Center style={{color: "#fff"}}>居中</Center>
          </Container>
        </Center>
      </Expanded>
    </Row>
  </Container>
</Playground>

### 实现左右平分，中间 20 间距

<Playground>
  <Container width="100%">
    <Row>
      <Expanded style={{background: "coral"}}>
        <Center>左边</Center>
      </Expanded>
      <Container width="20px" color="red">间距</Container>
      <Expanded style={{background: "coral"}}>
        <Center>右边</Center>
      </Expanded>
    </Row>
  </Container>
</Playground>
