## Column

提供垂直布局的组件。等价于 `flex-direction: column`

## 使用

<Playground>
  <Column>
    <Container width="100px" height="100px" color="#ececec">
      <Center>居中</Center>
    </Container>
    <Container width="100px" height="100px" color="blue">
      <Center style={{color: "#fff"}}>居中</Center>
    </Container>
  </Column>
</Playground>

### 上下左右布局

<Playground>
  <Container height="500px">
    <Column>
      <Container>
        <Center>Navbar</Center>
      </Container>
      <Row crossAxisSize="max">
        <Container width="100px" height="100%">
          <Center>Sidebar</Center>
        </Container>
        <Expanded>
          <Center>Main</Center>
        </Expanded>
      </Row>
    </Column>
  </Container>
</Playground>
